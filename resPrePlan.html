Research Preparation and Planning

This section explores the objectives and goals of research, methods for topic selection, critical thinking, scientific enquiry, hypothesis formulation, and proposal development for theoretical and experimental research.

Key Topics Overview

1. Objectives of Research: Understanding the purpose of exploration, explanation, and prediction.

2. Research Goals: Establishing clarity in purpose, contribution, and scientific reasoning.

3. Critical Thinking: Logical reasoning, evaluating evidence, and questioning assumptions.

4. Generating Research Topics: Idea generation through literature gaps and real-world problems.

5. Topic Selection & Justification: Choosing feasible, original, and significant problems.

6. Designing Questionnaires: Framing unbiased, clear, and valid data collection tools.

7. Scientific Enquiry: Observation → Hypothesis → Experimentation → Conclusion.

8. Hypothesis Testing: Using statistical methods to validate or refute assumptions.

9. Research Proposal Development: Structuring objectives, methods, and expected outcomes.

10. Theoretical & Experimental Processes: Connecting models, experiments, and validation.
