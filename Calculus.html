Calculus

A compact review of single- and multi-variable calculus: limits, differentiation, integration, sequences & series, Taylor expansions, partial derivatives, and elementary differential equations — all focused on applications in analysis and research.

Key Topics Explained

1. Limits & Continuity: Limit definitions, one-sided limits, and function continuity — the base for defining derivatives and integrals.

2. Differentiation: Definition via limits, derivative rules, maxima-minima, Taylor series expansion, and applications in physics and optimization.

3. Integration: Indefinite and definite integrals, Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, substitution, integration by parts, and area under curve.

4. Sequences & Series: Convergence, power series, and tests like ratio and comparison tests. Taylor and Maclaurin expansions.

5. Multivariable Calculus: Partial derivatives, gradients, Jacobians, multiple integrals, and vector calculus basics.

6. Differential Equations: First-order linear and separable ODEs, second-order linear equations, and their applications in system modeling.

7. Optimization: Lagrange multipliers and constraints, real-world optimization using calculus techniques.
