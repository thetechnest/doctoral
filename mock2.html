Research Methodology — Comprehensive Review

A complete overview of research preparation, data collection, academic writing, and mathematical modeling. This page summarizes Units I–V from the SRM PhD syllabus with topic notes and interactive MCQs.

Key Units Overview

Unit I — Research Preparation: Objectives, goals, critical thinking, topic generation, hypothesis formulation, proposal design.

Unit II — Research Resources: Sources of information, online databases, citation indices, impact factor, plagiarism, IPR, and patent laws.

Unit III — Academic Writing & Presentation: Proposal writing, funding agencies, oral/poster presentations, thesis, and report writing.

Unit IV — Data Collection & Analysis: Sampling, statistical distributions, hypothesis testing, regression, ANOVA, time-series, and design of experiments.

Unit V — Mathematical Modelling: Modeling techniques, simulation, validation, and optimization (GA, PSO, SA).
