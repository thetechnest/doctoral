Welcome to the PhD Entrance Exam Preparation Portal

This Preparation Guide serves as your academic companion to prepare effectively for the SRM University PhD Entrance Examination. The platform provides access to key modules in Research Methodology and Computer Science with supporting mock tests, detailed syllabus, and updated guidelines.

Our mission is to help each research aspirant build a strong foundation in critical thinking, scientific research methods, and proposal writing to ensure academic excellence and readiness for doctoral-level studies.

About the PhD Admission Examination

The PhD Entrance Test is designed to assess both your subject expertise and your aptitude for research. It is divided into two equally weighted sections:

  • Research Methodology: Covers objectives of research, hypothesis formulation, questionnaire design, methods of scientific enquiry, and research proposal development.
  • Computer Science Core: Focuses on data structures, algorithms, AI/ML basics, DBMS, and computer networks.

Examination Details

Date of Entrance Exam: 10th November 2025 (Monday)

Duration: 2 Hours (9.30 AM - 11.30 AM) – Reporting Time: 09.00 AM

Venue: Tech Park 1 – 3rd Floor, SRMIST, Kattankulathur Campus

Map: View on Google Maps

Type of Exam: Objective Type (Multiple Choice; No Negative Marking). 50% of questions are from Computer Science and 50% from Research Methodology.

Date of Interview: 10th November 2025 (Monday) from 12.00 PM for shortlisted candidates.

Instructions for Shortlisted Candidates:

  1. The interview will be conducted on the same day and will begin at 12.00 P.M.
  2. Candidates should present their proposed research within 10 minutes.
  3. Presentation should include scanned copies of ID proof, UG and PG certificates. Part-time external candidates must provide proof of employment.
  4. Research presentation will be allowed only after document verification.
